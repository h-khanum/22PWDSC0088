Welcome to FitFreaks' Workout Plans – designed to help you reach your fitness goals, whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete. Choose from a variety of plans tailored to boost strength, increase flexibility, improve endurance, and promote overall wellness.

Full Body Workout

This full body workout is designed to engage all major muscle groups. Ideal for beginners and experienced athletes alike.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Cardio Workout

This cardio routine is great for improving heart health and burning calories. Perfect for all fitness levels.

Exercises:

  • Running: 20 minutes
  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps

Strength Training

This strength training routine focuses on building muscle and enhancing strength through targeted exercises.

Exercises:

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
